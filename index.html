About Me

Hi! I'm Kassidy and I have a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science. I have a passion for human-centered design and have completed coursework in human-computer interaction, marketing, and psychology.

Colorado College has a unique academic system where students complete a class in three and a half weeks. At Colorado College, I've learned to think critically in a fast-paced environment. My coursework has given me a strong foundation in understanding the ways in which people interact with technology and pushed me to pursue a career in product design.

I worked as a DevOps Intern at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory where I learned about the importance of UX research in the design thinking process. Additionally, my customer service experience coupled with my natural inclination for aesthetics puts me in a position to both understand and deliver user-centric solutions. I am excited to apply my skills and passion for design to a career in product design with a commitment to continuous learning and improvement.

In my free time, I love cooking, playing sports such as soccer and basketball, and venturing into the outdoors through hiking, backpacking, or mountain biking. I capture these experiences with phone photography and painting.
