About Me

Hi! I'm Kassidy, a user-centered designer with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science. My academic foundation in human-computer interaction combined with practical experience in web development has fueled my passion for creating intuitive digital experiences that solve real user problems.

Throughout my education and professional experiences, I've developed a holistic approach to UX design. My studies in computer science gave me strong technical skills, while coursework in human-computer interaction, marketing, and psychology has helped me understand the crucial intersection between technology and human behavior. This interdisciplinary background allows me to bridge the gap between technical requirements and user needs.

As a Web Fellow at the USDA Southeast Climate Hub, I've implemented UI/UX principles to enhance user experiences and ensure accessibility compliance across multiple digital platforms. I regularly analyze Google Analytics data to inform design decisions and measure the impact of UX improvements. My experience maintaining content management systems has taught me how to work within established frameworks while identifying opportunities for enhanced usability.

My product design training through GoCreate USA's intensive bootcamp has strengthened my skills in user research, wireframing, and creating high-fidelity prototypes. In academic projects like "Burn By Beats," I applied the full design thinking process—from user research to interactive prototyping—demonstrating my ability to translate user needs into intuitive interfaces.

I bring technical versatility with skills in HTML/CSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, and experience with design tools like Figma. My background in data analysis through R programming enables me to approach design decisions with both creativity and analytical rigor. Through my DevOps internship at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, I gained valuable experience working with technical teams in an agile environment, reinforcing my belief in collaborative, iterative design processes.

I'm passionate about creating accessible, user-friendly experiences that make complex systems more intuitive and engaging. My goal as a UX designer is to combine empathy, data insights, and technical knowledge to craft digital solutions that truly enhance the user journey.
